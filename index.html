about founders and coders

Foundersand Coders is tuition-free, full stack JavaScript coding bootcamp with campuses in London, UK and Nazareth, Israel, Founders and Coders is not your ordinary coding bootcamp. founders and coders is on a mission to prove that for many people peer-led learning is the most effective and least expensive route into the tech industry. founders and coders no paid teaching staff. While founders and coders do employ a part-time facilitator who coordinates course logistics, the course is primarily “peer-led.” I will learn to code by pair programming with a partner, working on team projects, and from “near-peer” mentorship by recent graduates.
